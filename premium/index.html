Premium

We offer a premium subscription for those of you who want additional features. You can purchase premium on patreon

Note! If you get premium, you MUST join the discord server to get what you are paying for

Perks

  • Auto-disconnect is disabled
  • Autoreconnect
  • 3 simultaneous crosschats
  • Ability to edit embed format
  • Ability to edit webhook format
